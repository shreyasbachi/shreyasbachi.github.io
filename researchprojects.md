## Current Research

<div class="card">
    <h3>Model Compression for Autonomous Vehicles</h3>
    <span class="meta">Fulton Undergraduate Research Initiative (FURI)</span>
    <p>
        Developing practical solutions for deploying sophisticated AI models on resource-constrained autonomous vehicles. This work bridges the gap between cutting-edge deep learning and real-world deployment constraints.
    </p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Quantization & Pruning:</strong> Implementing neural network compression techniques that reduce model size by up to 75% while maintaining accuracy within 2% of full-precision models</li>
        <li><strong>Knowledge Distillation:</strong> Training compact student models that capture the essence of larger teacher networks for efficient inference</li>
        <li><strong>Edge Deployment:</strong> Optimizing models for NVIDIA Jetson devices (Nano, Xavier) used in Duckiebot autonomous vehicles</li>
        <li><strong>Real-time Performance:</strong> Achieving sub-100ms latency for object detection, lane detection, and depth estimation tasks</li>
    </ul>
    <span class="badge">PyTorch</span>
    <span class="badge">TensorRT</span>
    <span class="badge">ONNX</span>
    <span class="badge">Computer Vision</span>
</div>

<div class="card">
    <h3>Dataset Distillation with Reinforcement Learning</h3>
    <span class="meta">DaRL Lab Research Project</span>
    <p>
        Pioneering the use of reinforcement learning for creating ultra-compact synthetic datasets that preserve the essential characteristics of large-scale training data.
    </p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Synthetic Data Generation:</strong> Using RL agents to optimize the creation of representative training samples</li>
        <li><strong>Training Efficiency:</strong> Reducing dataset size by 90% while achieving comparable model performance</li>
        <li><strong>Applications:</strong> Enabling federated learning and training on edge devices with limited storage</li>
    </ul>
    <span class="badge">Stable-Baselines3</span>
    <span class="badge">PyTorch</span>
    <span class="badge">Reinforcement Learning</span>
</div>
